Our mission

It's our program for IT specialists who are interested in spending their free time to help people and make the world a better place. We work with NGOs, charity organizations, hospitals, schools, social initiatives, government bodies that need help. Our goal is not to get a commercial profit but contribute our free time on developing the solutions for those in need of help. Every hour you spend on coding, designing, managing or testing matters and every person who participates in social activities will be recognized for their contribution in the final case study.
