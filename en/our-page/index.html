National bone marrow donor register (Belarus)

The national bone marrow donor registry is used for the collection and analysis of data on bone marrow and stem cell donors and in the search for compatible donors for patients with serious hematological conditions requiring transplantation.

GENTL MASK

The GENTL Mask is an Open Source face mask design that utilizes readily available materials and incredibly simple manufacturing processes to enable localized manufacturing to help close the PPE supply gap during the COVID-19 pandemic.

QR Code Generator for school textbooks

Book code catalog is a web service that lets the Ministry of Education of the Republic of Belarus create, track and edit QR codes and short links for school textbooks.

The mobile app for firefighting team

We developed an application that helps firefighters to quickly and accurately calcsectionate how much time they can spend in a smoke-filled building before they run out of oxygen.

Rano.by: a digital platform for parents of premature babies

Rano.by is a digital platform for parents of premature babies founded by the Republican Public Association Rano. EPAM volunteers helped to improve the platform, create a responsive design and automate the collection of children's health data.

Republican kidney transplant waiting list (Belarus)

The republican kidney transplant waiting list collects and processes information about patients waiting for kidney transplants and facilitates and supports final decisions on the recipients of available organs.

DIIA digital state in smartphone

DIIA is an app that provides smartphone access to government services. It lets users download electronic documents (passport, driver's license, insurance, etc.) for use instead of the original hard copies.

Liver transplant waiting list (Belarus)

The Belarus liver transplant waiting list is a web application that automates the process of selecting patients for liver transplants.
